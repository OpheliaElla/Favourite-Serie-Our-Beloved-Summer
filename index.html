Our Beloved Summer (Korean: 그 해 우리는; RR: Geu Hae Urineun; lit. That Year We) is a South Korean romantic comedy television series. Billed as "Studio N's first original series",[2] it is directed by Kim Yoon-jin with screenplay by Lee Na-eun, starring Choi Woo-shik, Kim Da-mi, Kim Sung-cheol and Roh Jeong-eui.[3] The series is a coming-of-age story about a former couple who are forced to come together again when a documentary they shot in high school goes viral. It premiered on SBS TV on December 6, 2021, and aired on Mondays and Tuesdays at 22:00 (KST) till January 25, 2022. It is available for streaming on Netflix. Watch Now on Netflix
